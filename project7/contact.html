MORE ABOUT ME

I got into photography because of sports. I was a competitive swimmer for years and loved sports. When I started taking photos at swim meets and games, I realized I could combine my love of sports with photography.

Now I'm studying Art Photography at Syracuse University. My classes have taught me better techniques for lighting, composition, and editing. I've learned both the technical and creative sides of photography.

Getting hired by Syracuse Athletics was a big step for my career. Shooting college sports has taught me how to work under pressure and catch the right moments. Every game is different and teaches me something new.

I started doing food photography because it's similar to sports photography in some ways. Both need good timing, lighting, and composition. Both tell a story through images.

As a former athlete, I understand hard work and discipline. I show up prepared and get the job done right.

WHAT I OFFER

HOW IT WORKS

We Talk: First, we'll discuss what you need, when you need it, and what you're looking for.

I Send a Proposal: I'll send you details about what I'll do, when I'll do it, and how much it costs.

We Plan: For bigger projects, we'll plan out the details before the shoot.

Photo Shoot: I show up ready with all my equipment and take the photos.

Editing: I edit the photos to look their best.

You Get Your Photos: I send you the final high-quality images online where you can download them.

COMMON QUESTIONS

How soon should I book you?
Try to book at least two weeks ahead, but I can sometimes work with shorter notice.

Do I get the original files?
You get fully edited high-quality photos. Original raw files cost extra if you want them.

Can I use the photos however I want?
Yes, the photos are yours to use for your intended purpose.

Do you travel?
Yes! I'm based in Syracuse but can travel for projects.
