Sports and Food Photography

About Me

Hi, I'm Ben Thomas. I'm a photography student at Syracuse University studying Art Photography. I specialize in two types of photography: sports and food.

I currently work as a photographer for Syracuse Athletics, where I shoot games and take photos of athletes. I also do food photography for restaurants and businesses.

What I Do

Sports Photography

I photograph sporting events, games, and athletes. My work includes action shots during games, team photos, and individual athlete portraits. I have experience shooting basketball, football, soccer, and other sports for Syracuse Athletics.

Food Photography

I create photos of food for restaurants, menus, and social media. Whether it's a fancy plated dish or a casual burger, I make food look great through good lighting and composition.

Why Hire Me

I have real experience shooting for Syracuse Athletics at actual games and events. As a student in Syracuse's art photography program, I've learned professional techniques and skills. I'm reliable, communicate well, and deliver quality photos on time.
